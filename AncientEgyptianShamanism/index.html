Ancient Egyptian Shamanism

Course Modules

Module 1 covers what Egyptian shamanism is, what shamanism means, and the influence of ancient Egypt's spirituality on archaeology, Western occultism, the Kemet movement, and the world in general.

Module 2 is all about ancient Egyptian beliefs, rituals and practices, the three models of cosmology, the Egyptian pantheon of deities, Divine Rulership, and the fate of the soul after life.

Module 3 covers Egyptian mythology and its relevance and the ancient Egyptian culture of magical texts, rituals, festivals, oracles, magic, and spiritual items in the context of ancient Egyptian spiritual practices.

Module 4 discusses the role of priests in ancient Egypt, the Modern Shamanic Path, and ways to avoid cultural appropriation.

Module 5 looks at how visiting Egypt can connect you to ancient deities. This module also covers astral travel, the creation of altars, and the re-creation of Egyptian rituals from ancient times.

Module 6 and Module 7, you will discover ancient Egyptian deities. Topics include the deities of the Ennead, the relationships between them, and their roles in the mythological world of ancient Egypt.

Module 8 covers heka, including the four components of heka and the herbs and medicines used in conjunction with magic and shamanism.

Module 9 is dedicated to achieving trance states and increasing psychic powers, developing a relationship with ancient Egyptian spirits, and discovering the spiritual roots of illnesses and ways of healing them.

Module 10 looks at the Shaman Code of Ethics, physical and astral travel to Egypt, controversies in Egyptology, and the importance of the stars in ancient Egyptian spiritual practice.
